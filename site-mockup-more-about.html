ABOUT THE SITE

This website is a portfolio and site to keep track of my platforms and business. Through this site, I keep track of posted artwork, commission inquiries, character concepts, and general online activity. this site will also work as an archive in the future for my progress as an artist.

ABOUT ME

Hello! I go by Orchid, And this is my portfolio site. I am an autistic person with a huge passion for art and character creation. throughout the site there will be information and details on my projects and passions.

ABOUT MY WORK

In all my artwork you will see a heavy influence of large eyes and soft or muted colors. The majority of my work is inspired by dolls, puppets, vintage illustrations and a love of macabre in general. I am also very inspired by all things deemed unsettling and cute. I strive to find a solid balance between both traits and use it in my art accordingly.
